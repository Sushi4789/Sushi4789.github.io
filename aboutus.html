Welcome to SSC Hospital


The SSC Society's successful foray into healthcare began with the SSC Super specialty Hospital and Research Centre - a Hospital offering 2000 beds and state of the art medical facilities. The CSS Society continues the fine tradition of service through the SSC Hospital & Medical Research Centre, Bengaluru. This 2000 beds modern hospital envisioned by Dr. Suchet Rao Giri set on an 17 acre sprawling land mass also offers concessional beds to the poor and needy populace. The hospital provides the services of more than 300 consultant doctors, skilled nurses, technical and paramedical staff. The state of the art infrastructure includes 30 operation theatres and a wide range of specialty & super specialty features The leadership lineage dates back to 1995 wherein Colonel A K Singh, the harbinger took over the mantle of Medical Director on 02 May 95. This day on, the institution has heralded on its path of progress. On 06 Jan 2003, Dr MV Jali took over the reins of the hospital and successfully steered the development & research programs for more than two decades. The present incumbent Dr (Col) M Dayananada, a Military veteran assumed the appointment as Medical Director on 02 Nov 22 and with his stellar leadership has scaled this very Hospital & MRC on the National horizon. The SSC Hospital & MRC has achieved many distinctions in Cardiac and multi organ transplants. The US based “Smile Train Project” enables children with cleft - lip & palate and other dental deformities to benefit from surgery. The track record of the hospital is indicative of myriad appreciations & accolades with “A” Grade accreditation by NAAC. Recognizing the impetus of research in healthcare studies, the hospital provides clinical facilities to the students of the attached Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College under the aegis of SSC Academy of Higher Education & Research(KAHER) situated in the campus, which offers under graduate, post graduate and post-doctoral super specialty courses. The hospital has subsidiary health care centres at Kalaburagi,Mysuru, Belagavi and Ramnagar . In its endeavor to deliver quality healthcare to all sections of society , the hospital continues to set excellent standards and ensures that the SSC Hospital& MRC remains a role model that symbolizes the highest levels of healthcare at par with the best in the country.

DR. SUCHET RAO GIRI
chairman ,SSC TRUST.

Dr.Suchet Rao Giri is the chairman of SSC Association. He believed in 'Inclusive Excellence & Growth,' which revolutionized education and the development of modern India. Dr.Suchet Giri defines himself as “Destiny's Child,” and says that “giving back to society is his mission”. His vision in imparting education and qualitative research has resulted in the group collaborating with top premier universities across the world. He was awarded an Honorary Doctorate by Coventry University, United Kingdom, in 2006, and by the University of Illinois, Chicago, USA, in 2021, for his exemplary leadership role in promoting education and global research. To add another feather to his remarkable career, the Federation of Karnataka Chambers of Commerce and Industry (FKCCI) conferred him with the renowned “Sir M Visvesvaraya Memorial Award” in the presence of His Excellency Shri M Venkaiah Naidu, Hon’ble Vice President of India, in the year 2021.

DR. GURUDEV RAO GIRI
PRESIDENT,SSC TRUST.

SSC Hospital accredited by the NABH and NABL has a highly qualified, dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals who are committed to providing quality care affordably and most ethically to all sections of society. Backed by a state-of-the-art diagnostic laboratory, Radio-diagnosis, Radiotherapy, Dialysis & Robotics our team of specialists in various departments are “Ready to Serve”, at all times. Our core strengths include a state-of-the-art intensive care unit for adults, children, and neonates, teleconsultation, and an ever-responsive and alert accident and emergency team. Our hospital is a recognized center for various corporates, insurance, and government-sponsored health schemes. To keep pace with evolving trends in the health care facility we at SSC are in the process of continuously upgrading our technology & infrastructure. During the COVID pandemic, the hospital was at the forefront and took a lead in treating numerous COVID 19 patients across all sections of society.
